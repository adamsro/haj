Instructor Enrollment

Hawaiian Art Journey employs artists with proven teaching skills as well as expertise in the fine arts techniques. Classes may range from one to two weeks. Before applying we encourage you to browse our web site to familiarize yourself with our organization, programs, and community.

Deadline: Class proposal submissions are accepted on a rolling deadline.

Please contact Robert about instructor stipend.

Attachments: Please include 1) a resume or detailed listing of both your artistic and teaching experience; 2) three references (name, contact number, relationship); 3) web site or photographs of your work (no originals).
