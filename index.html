What is Hawaiian Art Journey?

Hawaiian Art Journey organizes one week art workshops held at the all inclusive Kona Village Resort taught by instructors renowned in their specialties; watercolorists, oil painters, pastel artists, sculptors, and anatomists.


Painting by the lagoon.

Why HAJ?

  1. Intensity We try for the most intense week of your year. All of your art activity should be to the good but this week is full focus, no distractions, great instruction, and full artistic friendship.
  2. Renewal Re-establish your goals. Find your personal balance. Gain direction. Take a week to do what needs to be done.
  3. Budget When you realize that this is an all inclusive week at an otherwise very expensive resort... well feel good about your savings.
  4. Plus, eat wonderfully, sleep as you yearn to sleep, be in nature. Find new friends of common interest; return home refreshed and energized. YOU CAN DO THIS!
