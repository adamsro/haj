Amenities

Included Amenities:

  • Fresh flower lei and tropical punch arrival greeting
  • Island-inspired breakfast, lunch and dinner served oceanfront
  • Wednesday & Friday evening Aha'aina - (lu'au) dinner & show
  • Tuesday evening Paniolo (cowboy) Night BBQ
  • Nightly entertainment at Bora Bora Bar
  • Wednesday evening General Manager's Cocktail Reception
  • Thursday evening Honeymooner's Sunset Mai Tai Reception (excluding holiday periods)
  • Boxed lunches or lunch voucher for select island restaurants
  • Coffee and pastries for early risers
  • Complimentary laundry facilities: washers, dryers and detergent
  • Complimentary self-parking
  • Concierge Services for in-house or island activity reservations
  • Internet access (from Concierge Services office only)
  • Lending library of books & novels

In-Room Amenities:

  • Adult-sized Bathrobes and slippers
  • 100% Kona Coffee plus selection of refrigerated soft drinks, juices & bottled water
  • Hair dryer
  • Iron and Ironing board
  • In-room Safe
  • Daily housekeeping service & Turndown service
  • Private Jacuzzi (applicable to certain Royal category units only)
  • Rooms cooled by tropical breezes and ceiling fans
  • Ambiance provided by chirping birds, crashing surf, rustling coconut fronds

Included Activities:

  • Use of tennis courts, racquets and balls, round robins and clinics
  • Use of Fitness facility including saunas
  • Ocean equipment such as snorkel gear, kayaks, sailboats*, boogie boards, surfboards (*orientation lesson required)
  • Arts and crafts such as shell hunting and lei making
  • Historical, botanical and petroglyph tours
  • Ping pong and Shuffleboard
  • Children's program for 5 - 12 year olds (Operates daily except May 1-25 and from September 2-30, 2009)
  • Hammocks scattered throughout the resort for rest & relaxation
  • Two fresh-water swimming pools

Optional Activities (fee applies)

  • Spa Services
  • Deep Sea Fishing
  • Outrigger Canoe* (orientation is required; unlimited usage thereafter)
  • Scuba Dive Excursions
  • Scuba Certification
  • Scooter (DPV) Snorkels or Dives
  • Private Tennis Lessons
  • Boutique
  • On-site Car Rental
  • Golf (nearby)
  • Horseback riding (nearby)
  • Helicopter Adventures (pickup on site)

*Inclusions may not apply to certain packages and special rates. Inquire at time of booking. Taxes and gratuities not included. Fees apply to private instruction and use of motorized water sports equipment.
