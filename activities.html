Activities


Please note: Some of the extra goodies, like the spa, whale watching,
      or diving carry an additional cost determined by The Kona Village.

The core activities are: sleep (to the sound of waves), wake, walk, draw, study, relax, eat, and rest. If you can find centering and renewal it will be time well spent.

However, there are as many activities as you might wish for beyond the core. Please check the Kona Village web site. Tennis, swimming, fitness center, sauna, sunning, acres to explore, history, walking, running, planning meals together, new friends, revisit a favorite book, two luaus, one BarB Q, time for art conversation and fellowship.

Full-on nature, no TV, radio, room phones or sundry electronics. No intrusions. Warm sun, cool breezes, fans and pools, sunsets to remember. Children's program except in September. Diving, riding and golf at additional charge.

Other Kona Village Activities:

  • Use of tennis courts, racquets and balls, round robins and clinics
  • Use of Fitness facility including saunas
  • Ocean equipment such as snorkel gear, kayaks, sailboats*, boogie boards, surfboards (*orientation lesson required)
  • Arts and crafts such as shell hunting and lei making
  • Historical, botanical and petroglyph tours
  • Ping pong and Shuffleboard
  • Children's program for 5 - 12 year olds (Operates daily except May 1-25 and from September 2-30, 2009)
  • Hammocks scattered throughout the resort for rest & relaxation
  • Two fresh-water swimming pools

Optional Activities (fee applies)

  • Spa Services
  • Deep Sea Fishing
  • Outrigger Canoe* (orientation is required; unlimited usage thereafter)
  • Scuba Dive Excursions
  • Scuba Certification
  • Scooter (DPV) Snorkels or Dives
  • Private Tennis Lessons
  • Boutique
  • On-site Car Rental
  • Golf (nearby)
  • Horseback riding (nearby)
  • Helicopter Adventures (pickup on site)
