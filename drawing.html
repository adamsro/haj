Drawing

The twentieth century saw a movement away from the study of drawing. You could go to art school and later practice art without any real foundation in drawing. Now among practicing artists the move is strongly back to drawing. There will be open drawing groups in differing locations with various models. I hope everyone will take time to participate.

I recommend reading Peter Steinhart's book which is to be found on the links page. There will be many masters, many disciplines, and many points of view. But it is my hope that we can come together in our enjoyment of the location and the moment and in our love of art.
