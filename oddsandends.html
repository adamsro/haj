Odds and Ends

You can cruise in or fly in. All travel is the responsibility of the individual. Please advise us of your arrival so that we may meet you. Book early, as soon as you know your class is a "go". By and large rates will not get better in the future. I have enjoyed Vickie Kiebler of Punahele Travel 877-320-0647(toll free) or vtavel@hawaiiantel.net Carry travel (trip) insurance, stuff happens and low price tickets are generally not refundable.

As to packing, if flying, pack light. Days are informal and dinner slightly less so. Dress shorts or pareos for either gender. Collared shirts and shoes. So called "resort wear" During the day, practical and cool, include swim suits and a shirt to paint in. We have a resort boutique for sun block and such.

There are two dining rooms and two bars open at varying times. There is also a very good art store in town. Call them a month in advance for quantities, specialties or bulk delivery. Tink&Ink, (808)326-2820
