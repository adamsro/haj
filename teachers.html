All the pictures, descriptions and such on this site might leave you thinking that this is a wonderful vacation. It is, but that is the least of it. What you learn and what happens within you is far more important. This is meant to be a time of reflection and growth. This growth is accompanied by and accomplished with learning, through practice and instruction. The instruction is vital. Take time to review the work and careers of the teachers listed here. Because of who and where we are, I am attracting some of the best instructors in the nation!

Here are my requirements: That the instructor is an excellent and accomplished artist, that they have a demonstrated commitment to teaching, and that they have a website capable of introducing themselves and their ideas. Dedication and a good sense of humor are important! People who meet these criteria deserve your close attention.
