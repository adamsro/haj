Anthony Holdsworth

Urban oil landscapes

Gallery scrolls! Click to enlarge.

In Orwel's "Animal Farm" it is said that all animals are equal but some are more equal than others.  Likewise. all artists are unique but Anthony Holdsworth is one of a kind.  He is the first to join HAJ and he brings with him a wonderful background in teaching.  He works closely with each student to define and achieve their goals.  He seems to know what to say or demonstrate to help you advance quickly.  However, there is nothing that I can say here that is not better shown on his web site.  The site is set up by his son (just as my son Marshall does this site) but I think it reflects Anthony perfectly.  He has a worthwhile blog attached.  Call either of us with questions but if at all possible attend.  I know I am looking forward to his visit.
