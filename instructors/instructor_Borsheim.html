Kelly Borsheim

Anatomist; Sculpts, Paints, Draws - Wow

Gallery scrolls! Click to enlarge.

Now some of the people I encounter have spent a lifetime in order to do something well. Kelly Borsheim, it seems, has spent a lifetime in order to do everything well. She is best known as a sculptor. When you see her web site you can admire her work and her success in placing sculptures, various easel arts, and wait!, Pastels!

I need a pastel maven, so after much talk and email Kelly is coming to Hawaii. Now teaching and travel are not new to her. Check out her travels in Italy. All I can do in these brief intros is encourage you to spend some time getting to know these fine artists. In this case Kelly has put up a separate web page for HAJ. I am expecting people from all over the country. Not only is she very accomplished but she is also very accessible, all in all, it is a nice package and not to be missed.
