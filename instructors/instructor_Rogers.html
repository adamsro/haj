Steve & Janet Rogers

Landscape & Portrait Watercolors

Gallery scrolls! Click to enlarge.

Notice: Steve will becoming to teach, Janet has scheduled knee surgery.

Well... Steve and Janet Rogers met years ago at a watercolor workshop. Now I cannot promise you romance and lifetime happiness from Hawaiian Art Journey, however there's an excellent chance that you will be challenged and advanced in your own journey by taking this class. So popular are the Rogers that it has been difficult to find a time where I can get them together for an entire week. Only by promising them tropical paradise in the middle of winter fourteen months before the fact have we been able to get this class to happen.

Now while they differ in their art and their approach they have team taught together before. They have promised me one of the year's most exciting painting experiences. We always have the scenery that Steve thrives on but for Janet we are assembling a weeks worth of our best models. If you sign up early, and I do expect this class to sell out, I will try and make this a winter annual.
