Brenda Beck Fisher

Teacher Supreme

Gallery scrolls! Click to enlarge.

I have contacted, as can be seen, many master painters. Brenda Fisher, however, has been the first one who is enthusiastic about teaching beginners. The importance of how you start out cannot be over emphasized. With Brenda you not only get a sound grounding, but you are inspired as well. This is a class that will give you a running start at watercolors. We hope that once you attend you will stay in touch with Brenda and Hawaiian Art Journey, and we will be able to foster growth for a lifetime.
