Penny Gupton

Paint the Hidden Kona Village

Gallery scrolls! Click to enlarge.

Every artist is singular but Penny is also singular to Kona Village. Most people studying here come to work intensely with an instructor. Given the location it is just as valid to come and paint the area. With Penny you can have it both ways. No one has painted here longer or with more verve. Penny has been here for years before Hawaiian Art Journey. She has helped countless people with their tropical art work. She knows the secrets of the location and the joy of it's light. Please check her web site. If you ever thought you might want to run away and paint in the tropics, now is the time, this is the place and Penny is your guide.
