Thomas W. Schaller

Architectural & Landscape Watercolor

Gallery scrolls! Click to enlarge.

If described Thomas Schaller's watercolors would seem perfectly straight forward but when you see them or see only his client list you realize you are in the presence of something exceptional. Individuals and companies from around the world have sought his work to express their highest aspirations. Similarly his fine art work is also distinctive and exceptional. Here is where seeing is better then description. What he does many attempt but few achieve. Because of his schedule it will be difficult to have him here as much as I would like. Look at his work and take advantage of this opportunity.
